SLAC Hackathon


Our Idea

There are plenty of resources that we see getting wasted every single day. Though there exists a good number of people who could use the items that we conveniently throw away, the gap between the donor and recipient often leads to wastage. This was an issue that we wanted to target.

Through our application, we aimed to connect donors and recipients of various articles like books, clothes or even perishable goods like food. The app would find the nearest target audience based on your location and would notify the user, so that he can accept the request.

Although this app still has a far way to go, we did learn quite a bit during the 24-hout period. Bonding over pizzas and scrounging for coding resources overnight, it was a great learning experience for all of us!
