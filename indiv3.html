My Experience

At the application stage, candidates were asked to vividly describe an idea for their upcoming project. I had chosen to develop a competitive pricing app - an application that shows prices, reviews and offers from various e-commerce sites like Amazon, Flipkart etc. I had heard that Ruby on Rails is one of the most convenient frameworks to build such applications, which is why I was keen on learning it.

Learn IT Girl introduced me to concepts like MVC architecture, web-scraping and database management systems. Also, one of the greatest advantages was receiving individual attention from the mentor, which helped us learn faster.

LITG was an amazingly fun and engaging experience for me. Working with a mentor taught me how to stick to deadlines, manage time efficiently and write clean code, to name a few. I must say that this program is a great initiative and I would love to be part of this community in the coming years.
